Our Menu

Major Bakery – Baking Joy Since 1984

Bringing you the taste of tradition and freshness every single day. From rich layered cakes to crispy cookies and savory snacks, we create every bite with love and quality.

  • Freshly baked cakes made with premium ingredients and artistic care.
  • Buttery cookies, crunchy and delightful – perfect for every occasion.
  • Traditional Indian sweets, biscuits, and homemade snacks that remind you of home.
  • Trusted by the community since 1984 for taste, freshness, and friendly service.

At Major Bakery, we believe in delivering not just food, but happiness. Every product is crafted in-house, ensuring quality, hygiene, and unbeatable flavor. Whether it's a birthday, a festival, or just a craving — we’ve got the perfect treat waiting for you!
